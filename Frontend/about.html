About Us

Upwardly is an Web Application created under the E-Summit Hackathon MANIT Bhopal. This project is made under the problem statement of Campus Placement Platform for Higher Educational Institution. The main objective of this application is to help T&P Cells for different universities. This application overall helps student in preparing for their upcoming Placements. Upwardly also provides different essential resources, Insigts, Placement Preparation and Quizes.
